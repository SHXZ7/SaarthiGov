## ELIGIBILITY

Unemployment Allowance Scheme (UAS) - Basic Eligibility:
- Must be registered in Employment Exchange in Kerala
- Minimum registration seniority: 3 years after completing 18 years of age
- Age limits: 18 years (lower) to 35 years (upper)
- Annual family income must not exceed Rs. 12,000
- Personal monthly income must not exceed Rs. 100 per month
- Students are NOT eligible

Educational Qualifications:
- General category: SSLC pass required
- SC/ST candidates: Must have at least appeared for SSLC examination after regular schooling
- Physically handicapped: Must have attended SSLC examination after regular schooling, with continuous registration seniority of 2 years after attaining 18 years of age

MGNREGA Unemployment Allowance Eligibility:
- Must be registered under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme
- Must have applied for work and not been provided employment within prescribed time (15 days)
- Must be able to produce dated receipt acknowledging the application for employment
- Application must be registered at Grama Panchayat and entered in Management Information System (MIS)

Disentitlement Conditions (MGNREGA):
- Refusal of offered work
- Not reporting for work when employment is provided
- After receiving 100 days of employment in a financial year (household)
- Circumstances specified in Section 9 of MGNREGA

## REQUIRED_DOCUMENTS

Documents for Unemployment Allowance Scheme (UAS):
- Prescribed application form
- Employment Exchange registration card/certificate
- Proof of registration seniority (3 years after age 18)
- Educational qualification certificate (SSLC or equivalent)
- Income certificate (annual family income below Rs. 12,000)
- Age proof
- Address proof

Documents for MGNREGA Unemployment Allowance:
- Form No.1: Application for Unemployment Allowance
- Form No.2: Copy of acknowledgement/receipt for employment application
- Job Card with Job Card Number
- Aadhaar Number
- Bank account details (account number and bank name)
- Details of days already worked during the year
- Proof of wages received in bank account

Information Required in Form No.1:
- Name and Address
- Job Card Number
- Aadhaar Number
- Bank name and account number
- Number of days of job already received during the year
- Amount received as unskilled wages
- Date on which job was demanded
- Number of days of job demanded
- Whether advance date was requested (Yes/No)
- Whether dated receipt for job demand is attached (Yes/No)

## APPLICATION_PROCESS

Unemployment Allowance Scheme (UAS) - Application Process:
1. Submit application in prescribed form to concerned Local Body (Grama Panchayat / Municipality / Corporation)
2. Application can be submitted at any time as per revised norms
3. Applications are verified by concerned Employment Exchanges
4. Welfare Standing Committee of Local Body examines and sanctions or rejects on merit
5. Eligible candidate receives allowance from the month following sanction
6. Disbursement subject to authorization and fund availability

MGNREGA Unemployment Allowance - Application Process:
1. First, apply for employment at Grama Panchayat (oral or written application)
2. Obtain dated receipt (Form No.2) acknowledging employment application
3. Wait for 15 days from date of employment application
4. If employment not provided within 15 days, submit Form No.1 to Grama Panchayat Secretary on the next day after 15th day
5. Attach copy of Form No.2 receipt with the application
6. Grama Panchayat Secretary verifies claim by cross-checking:
   - Job Card Register
   - Employment Register
   - Management Information System
   - Muster Rolls
7. If satisfied, Secretary forwards application with remarks to Programme Officer (Block level)
8. Programme Officer appraises facts and issues orders
9. Payment credited to worker's bank account through electronic fund management system

Allowance Rates (MGNREGA):
- First 30 days in financial year: 1/4 (one-fourth) of daily wage rate for unskilled workers
- Remaining period of financial year: Not less than 1/2 (one-half) of daily wage rate for unskilled workers

Benefit Amount (UAS):
- Rs. 120 per month as unemployment dole

## SPECIAL_CASES

Appeal Process (UAS):
- If application rejected by Local Body, applicant can appeal to District Collector
- Appeal must be submitted within 60 days of receiving rejection memo

Appeal Process (MGNREGA):
- If Programme Officer rejects unemployment allowance claim, reasons must be recorded and intimated in writing
- Aggrieved person can file written appeal in Form No.3 to District Programme Co-ordinator
- Appeal must be filed within 30 days of receiving Programme Officer's order
- District Programme Co-ordinator must dispose of appeal within 15 days after summary enquiry

Rejection of Claim:
- Programme Officer can reject unemployment allowance only on grounds of force majeure
- All other rejections must follow due process

Recovery Process:
- If appellate authority orders recovery, Grama Panchayat recovers amount
- Recovery can be in lump sum or equal monthly installments
- From salary (permanent employees) or wages (contract employees)
- Revenue recovery process may be initiated if needed

Payment Restrictions:
- No cash payments permitted for MGNREGA unemployment allowance
- Payment only through Direct Benefit Transfer to verified bank account
- Cannot be paid from Central or State funds meant for regular MGNREGA implementation

Physically Handicapped Persons:
- Special eligibility: 2 years registration seniority (instead of 3 years)
- Must have attended SSLC examination after regular schooling

SC/ST Candidates:
- Relaxed educational requirement: Only need to have appeared for SSLC (not necessarily passed)

Cessation of Liability:
- State Government's liability to pay unemployment allowance ceases after 100 days of employment provided to household in financial year
- Also ceases in other circumstances as per Section 7(3) of MGNREGA

## GOVERNMENT_RULES

Unemployment Allowance Scheme (UAS) - Legal Framework:
- Introduction: G.O.(P) No.40/82/LBR dated 12/11/1982
- Transfer to Local Bodies: G.O.(P) No.23/98/LBR dated 28/05/1998
- Controlling Authority: Director of Employment
- Implementing Agencies: Grama Panchayats, Municipalities, Corporations

MGNREGA Unemployment Allowance Rules, 2021:
- Title: Kerala Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme Unemployment Allowance Rules, 2021
- Notification: G.O.(P) No.11/2021/LSGD, S.R.O. No.129/2021
- Date of Notification: 05 February 2021
- Came into force: Immediately from 05 February 2021
- Issuing Authority: Government of Kerala, Local Self Government (DD) Department
- Legal Basis: Section 32(1) of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act, 2005 (Central Act 42 of 2005)

Key Definitions (MGNREGA Rules):
- "Act": Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act, 2005 (Central Act 42 of 2005)
- "Block": Community development area within a district comprising group of Grama Panchayats
- "Central Rules": Rules framed by Government of India under MGNREGA
- "District Programme Coordinator": District Collector designated for scheme implementation
- "Panchayat Raj Act": Kerala Panchayat Raj Act, 1994 (Act 13 of 1994)
- "Programme Officer": Officer not below rank of Block Panchayat Secretary, appointed as Block Programme Officer
- "Joint Programme Co-ordinator": District level officer of Rural Development Department assisting District Programme Co-ordinator
- "State Cell": NREGA Cell constituted under MGNREGA by Government of Kerala
- "Wage rate for Unskilled Workers": Daily wage rate fixed by Central Government for unskilled workers under MGNREGA

Social Security Pension Related Orders:
- G.O.(MS) 408/2017/Fin dated 30/08/2017: Social Security Pension distribution via cooperative credit societies
- G.O.(MS) 67/2017/Fin dated 06/02/2017: Universal Social Security Pension streamlining
- G.O.(MS) 34/2017/Fin dated 21/01/2017: Eligibility conditions modification for Social Security Pensions
- GO (Ms) No.282/2016/Fin: Enhancement of minimum pension and limiting entitlement
- G.O.(P)No.1/97/SWD dated 01/01/1997: Social Security Pension rate enhancement

Forms Used:
- Form No.1: Application for Unemployment Allowance (sub-rule 1 of rule 4)
- Form No.2: Acknowledgement receipt for employment application
- Form No.3: Appeal form to District Programme Co-ordinator

Monitoring and Reporting:
- Director of Employment is controlling authority for UAS
- District Employment Officers inspect Local Body accounts
- Regular audits conducted
- Grama Panchayat Secretary submits monthly statement in Annexure register to Programme Officer
- Monthly register contains: worker name, period and days of allowance, rate, total amount, reasons for not providing work
