Here is concise, Kerala-specific raw data you can directly structure as a dataset for training.
​

A. Eligibility
who_can_apply:

"parent (mother or father)"

"guardian (if parents unavailable or deceased)"

"authorized person from hospital/institution (for institutional births)"

"head of household/nearest relative (for home births)"
​

who_can_register_event_information:

"for domiciliary/home births: head of house or nearest relative present"

"for institutional births: medical officer / person in charge of hospital, health centre, maternity home, nursing home"
​

jurisdiction_local_body:

"Gram Panchayat (rural areas)"

"Municipality (smaller urban towns)"

"Municipal Corporation (cities)"

"Registrar of Births & Deaths of the local body where birth occurred"

"registration unit is tied to local self government institution area"
​

B. Registration Timelines
normal_registration_period:

"information must be given within 21 days from date of birth"
​

late_registration_22_to_30_days:

"allowed with payment of late fee: ₹2 (Kerala rules)"

"no special written permission needed within 30 days; treated as delayed within prescribed extension"
​

late_registration_31_days_to_1_year:

"requires written permission of prescribed local authority (e.g., Registrar / higher officer)"

"late fee: ₹5 (Kerala rules)"

"affidavit by parents or informant explaining delay"
​

very_late_registration_after_1_year:

"requires order of First Class Magistrate / SDM (magistrate of first class or presidency magistrate)"

"late fee: ₹10 (Kerala rules)"

"supporting documents: school records, hospital records, other proofs, affidavits"
​

C. Documents Required
documents_hospital_birth:

"proof of birth from hospital (discharge summary / birth report on hospital letterhead)"

"hospital birth report in prescribed format (Form 1 – Birth Report legal + statistical part)"

"ID proof of parents (Aadhaar / voter ID / passport etc.)"

"address proof of parents (ration card / utility bill / Aadhaar address etc.)"

"parents’ birth certificates (if available, often requested but not strictly mandatory in all cases)"

"parents’ marriage certificate (if available)"

"details required: place of birth, date and time, sex of child, name of child (or option to add later), names of father and mother"
​

documents_home_birth:

"domiciliary birth report filled by head of household / nearest relative"

"certificate / letter from doctor / nurse / trained midwife / ASHA / traditional birth attendant confirming home delivery (if attended)"

"proof of residence within local body (address proof)"

"ID proofs of parents"

"any medical record if mother/child taken to hospital after delivery (optional but useful)"
​

documents_late_registration_general:

"standard birth registration form (Form 1) with complete details"

"proof of birth: hospital discharge summary OR doctor’s certificate OR vaccination card OR school admission record (for older child) OR religious ceremony record where date of birth is mentioned"

"ID proof of applicant/parents"

"address proof of parents at time of birth (if available) and current address"

"affidavit explaining reason for delay (mandatory after 30 days; format per local body/SDM)"

"permission letter from Registrar (31 days–1 year)"

"court/magistrate order (after 1 year)"
​

D. Application Process
online_process_CRS_Sevana:

"portal: Civil Registration System (CRS) integrated with Kerala Sevana (https://cr.lsgkerala.gov.in style endpoints; exact URL via Sevana)"

"step 1: user visits Sevana – 'Registration of Births & Deaths' module"

"step 2: choose 'New Registration' or 'Citizen services' depending on flow (institution vs citizen)"

"step 3: fill online birth registration details: child’s name (optional), date of birth, time of birth, sex, place (hospital/home), parents’ details, address"

"step 4: upload / link hospital generated birth report or provide hospital ID where hospital has pre-registered event"

"step 5: submit form; system routes to local Registrar of Births & Deaths of relevant Panchayat/Municipality/Corporation"

"step 6: after approval, certificate available for download via 'Certificate Search' using district, local body type, local body, year of birth, and child/parents details"
​

offline_process_local_body:

"applicant visits local Registrar of Births & Deaths in Gram Panchayat / Municipality / Corporation where birth occurred"

"collect or download physical Birth Report form (Form 1)"

"fill legal part (date of birth, sex, child name, parents’ names, place of birth) and statistical part (mother’s residence, religion, education of parents, etc.)"

"attach required documents (hospital certificate / supporting proof, ID, address, etc.)"

"submit to Registrar; pay applicable fee (normal registration usually free, late fees as per delay rules)"

"Registrar records entry in Birth Register (Form 7) and assigns registration number and date"

"extract (birth certificate) issued in Form 5; can be collected in person or sent by post"
​

E. Where to Apply
where_to_apply_local_self_government:

"Secretary/Registrar of Births & Deaths of:

Gram Panchayat for rural areas

Municipality for towns

Municipal Corporation for cities"

"jurisdiction is based on place of occurrence of birth, not permanent address"
​

where_to_apply_hospitals_institutional_births:

"for institutional births, initial reporting often done by hospital to local Registrar"

"parents may:

submit details at hospital help-desk / PRO for electronic registration, OR

collect extract from hospital liaison if integrated with local body, OR

later visit local body/Sevana portal using hospital registration number"
​

F. Special Cases
special_case_home_birth:

"event category: domiciliary/home birth"

"informant: head of household / nearest relative present"

"place of birth field: 'House' with full address"

"may require confirmation from local health worker / ASHA / Anganwadi / village officer for verification"
​

special_case_adoption:

"adopted child’s birth must already be registered at place of occurrence; adoption does not change original birth entry"

"for new certificate in adoptive name, process generally involves:

court adoption order or CARA recognized adoption deed

application to local Registrar with adoption order seeking change of child’s name and/or parents’ names as per legal direction

Registrar acts based on court directions; may issue new certificate without disclosing adoption status in routine extracts"
​

special_case_name_correction_or_addition:

"birth can be initially registered without child’s name"

"parent/guardian can provide name within 12 months from date of registration without court order; Registrar updates register and certificate"

"after 12 months, name entry/change allowed with late fee and supporting declaration as per section 14 and state rules"

"for spelling mistakes/clerical errors in name: Registrar can correct clerical/formal errors on enquiry and record correction in register; informant receives intimation"
​

special_case_date_or_major_detail_correction:

"if entry is erroneous in substance (e.g., wrong date of birth, wrong parents name), informant must produce declaration by two credible persons who know facts"

"Registrar may correct entry as per section 15 after verifying declaration and evidence"

"fraudulently or improperly made entries are reported to higher authority; action taken under section 25"
​

special_case_duplicate_certificate:

"if original certificate copy is lost, citizen can apply for additional certified extract under section 17"

"applicant provides: name, date of birth, place of birth, parents’ names, approximate year, and ID proof"

"Registrar conducts search in Birth Register (search fee per year, typically around ₹2 per year + certificate fee ₹5 as per Kerala rules; actual tariffs may be revised)"

"if entry found, Registrar issues another certified extract (Birth Certificate – Form 5); can be collected in person or by post"

"if entry not found, Registrar issues Non-Availability Certificate (Form 10)"
