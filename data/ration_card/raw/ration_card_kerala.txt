To be eligible for a ration card in Kerala, you must fulfill the following criteria:

You must be an Indian citizen

You must be a resident of the State of Kerala (permanent resident)

You must not hold any other Indian state's ration card

CARD TYPES EXPLANATION
​
Kerala issues different types of ration cards based on economic status:

1. Priority Card (Pink/Red Card) - Below Poverty Line (BPL)

For families with annual income less than ₹24,200

Provides 5 kg of food grains free of charge monthly

Designated for families living below the poverty line

2. Non-Priority Card (White Card) - Above Poverty Line (APL)

For families with annual income more than ₹100,000

Eligible for 2 kg of rice at subsidized rates

Designated for families living above the poverty line

APPLICATION INSTRUCTIONS
​
Online Application Process:
Visit the state civil supplies department's official webpage at www.civilsupplieskerala.gov.in

Click on the 'New Ration Card' option in Citizen Login

Answer if applying for a new ration card (Yes/No)

Select your Taluk Supply Office (TSO) from the dropdown menu

Enter valid information in all required fields (User Login ID, Password, Confirm Password, Name, Email, Mobile Number)

Solve the Captcha and click 'SUBMIT'

An activation link will be sent to your registered email ID - click this link to activate your account

Upload all required documents in the specified format (Passport size photo as JPG - maximum 15 KB; Documents as PDF - maximum 200 KB)

Review the form and click 'Print'

Sign the printed application form

Upload the signed application form and click 'FINAL SUBMIT'

The state government will issue your ration card within 15 days of receiving the payment

Required Documents:
​
Duly filled and signed application form

Ward Councillor's certificate (for residency verification)

Birth certificate or Secondary School Leaving Certificate (SSLC book)

Proof of identity (Aadhaar card, PAN card, Voter ID, Driving licence, or Passport)

Proof of address (Utility bills, Passport, Aadhaar card, or Rental agreement)

Passport-sized photograph of the head of the family

Aadhaar number linked with the ration card (for existing cardholders applying online)

Alternative - Offline Application:
Visit the nearby Taluk Supply Officer (TSO) or District Supply Officer (DSO) centre, fill up the application form, submit along with relevant documents for verification and processing.
