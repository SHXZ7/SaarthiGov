# Ration Card – Kerala (PDS)

## Eligibility
- Applicant must be an Indian citizen and a permanent resident of Kerala.
- The family must not hold a ration card issued by any other Indian state.
- The head of the family applies on behalf of all household members.
- Applicant must reside at the address for which the ration card is requested, supported by local residence proof and ward councillor / local body certificate.
- Eligibility for priority categories (AAY/PHH) is decided as per NFSA guidelines and Kerala Civil Supplies Department norms notified from time to time.

## Card Categories
- **AAY (Antyodaya Anna Yojana):**
  - For the poorest and most vulnerable households.
  - Includes destitute families and households with very low or no regular income.
  - Identified by the state under NFSA inclusion criteria.

- **PHH (Priority Household):**
  - For economically disadvantaged households meeting NFSA and Kerala state norms.
  - Includes low-income and other vulnerable groups identified by the Civil Supplies Department.

- **NPHH (Non-Priority Household):**
  - For families not eligible under AAY or PHH categories.
  - Issued mainly as an identity and address proof with limited or no subsidised benefits.

## Documents Required
**Mandatory documents (typical):**
- Filled ration card application form (online or prescribed offline form).
- Proof of identity of head of family and members (Aadhaar card; alternatively voter ID, PAN, driving licence, or passport).
- Proof of address in Kerala (Aadhaar with Kerala address, electricity/water/gas bill, rental agreement, passport, etc.).
- Birth certificate or SSLC book for age/date of birth proof of family members.
- Passport-size photograph of the head of family.
- Ward councillor / Grama Panchayat certificate confirming residence.

**Optional / situation-specific documents:**
- Income certificate issued by Village Office or competent authority (for AAY/PHH eligibility where required).
- Marriage certificate (for adding spouse or forming a new family).
- Transfer or relocation proof when shifting from another district or state.
- Death certificate (for deletion of a family member).
- Disability certificate or other vulnerability proofs, if applicable under state criteria.

## Application Process
1. Apply online through the Kerala Civil Supplies Department e-Citizen Ration Card Management System (RCMS) portal or visit an Akshaya centre.
2. Enter family and address details, select ration card category, provide Aadhaar numbers, and upload required documents.
3. Application is forwarded to the concerned Taluk Supply Office (TSO) / District Supply Office (DSO) for verification and approval; once approved, the ration card or printable e-ration card is issued.

## Where to Apply
- **Online:**
  - e-Citizen – Ration Card Management System (RCMS), Civil Supplies Department, Government of Kerala  
  - https://ecitizen.civilsupplieskerala.gov.in

- **Offline:**
  - Akshaya Centres across Kerala.
  - Taluk Supply Offices (TSO) / District Supply Offices (DSO).

## Special Cases
- **New family / family split:** Newly married couples or families forming a separate household can apply for a new ration card with marriage certificate or family split proof and residence certificate.
- **Address change / relocation:** Address corrections or transfers within Kerala require new address proof and ward certificate; shifting from another state may require surrender proof of the old ration card.
- **Duplicate ration card:** For lost or damaged cards, apply for a duplicate through the portal or Akshaya centre with ID proof and any affidavit or declaration required by the TSO.
- **Adding or removing members:** Member inclusion or deletion can be requested online or via Akshaya with supporting documents such as birth certificate, marriage certificate, or death certificate.
